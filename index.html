About Me

Hi, I'm Jodi! Some refer to me as a Creative Strategist, a Marketing Specialist, a Model, or a Content Creator but let's just say I'm an all-around Multi-Hyphenate. I am a dynamic multi-hyphenate creator on the rise, creating content specializing in Fashion, Lifestyle, and Travel. With a keen eye for style and an adventurous spirit, I curate content that resonates with a diverse audience. My work is distinguished by a dedicated focus on illuminating the black experience by curating safe spaces, both IRL and virtually. My work offer a window into worlds where young black creatives can envision themselves thriving and belonging.
